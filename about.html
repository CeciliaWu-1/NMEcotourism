關於我們

關於我們

我們是香港大學環境管理理學碩士課程的學生，正在組織一個名為「推廣北部都會區生態旅遊」的計畫。我們的目標之一，是為參與者提供導覽服務，讓他們體驗新的生態旅遊路線並收集回饋。收集到的見解將幫助我們提出策略建議，解決該地區現有的缺陷，並增強該地區的旅遊吸引力。

專案目標

過去幾年，乘客的出行習慣和偏好發生了多方面的變化。他們更喜歡深度的體驗，也更重視永續性。因此，有必要探索發展香港具有旅遊潛力的綠色景點。

本研究為配合政府所訂北部都會區藍綠康樂旅遊生態圈的發展計畫目標，將探討區域內生態旅遊產業潛力，推動北部都會區的生態旅遊，並為其提供策略建議，以解決北部都會區旅遊產業及相關配套的現存不足，使之成為知名旅遊勝地。最終旨在於香港推廣生態旅遊理念及加強綠色旅遊產品的推廣。

About Us

We are students from the Hong Kong University Master of Science in Environmental Management program, and we are organizing a project titled "Promoting Eco-tourism in the Northern Metropolis." We aim to provide guided tours for participants to experience the new eco-tour route and gather feedback. The insights collected will help us offer strategic recommendations to address the existing shortcomings of the area, also enhance the area's tourism appeal.

Project Aims

In the past few years, passengers' travel habits and preferences have changed in many aspects. They prefer in-depth experiences and are more aware of sustainability. Therefore, it is necessary to explore the development of green attractions in Hong Kong that have tourism potential.

To align with the goals of the development plan for the Northern Metropolis Blue and Green Recreation, Tourism, and Conservation Circle established by the government, this project aims to explore the potential of the eco-tourism industry within the region, promote ecotourism in the Northern Metropolis, and also offering strategic recommendations to address the existing shortcomings of the area and turn it as a well-known tourism destination. Eventually, aims to promote the concept of ecotourism in Hong Kong and strengthen the promotion of green tourism products.
